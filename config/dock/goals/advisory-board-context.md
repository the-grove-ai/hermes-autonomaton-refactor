## The Apple Lineage Angle

All three advisors share Apple heritage. This is not coincidental —
the Apple generation understood that coherent products are expressions
of coherent worldviews. That tradition went underground when the
industry optimized for speed over philosophy. Grove is its return in
the AI era.

The colophon format (intentional, explainable design decisions)
resonates strongly with this audience.
