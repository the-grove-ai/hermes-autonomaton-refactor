# Influencer Outreach

A strategic goal in service of GRV-001 — reach through aligned voices.

## What it is

Identify and engage five public voices whose audiences overlap the Grove
thesis (sovereign architecture, model independence, humane AI), and bring
them into the conversation on their own terms.

## How to operate

- Drafts go through the `jim-voice-writing-style` skill — strategy-consultant
  register, lead with the so-what, no sales voice.
- Specific over generic: reference the person's actual work, not a template.
- The ask is engagement on the thesis, not a favor.

## Priority voices

- **Cory Doctorow** — sovereignty, enshittification, user agency. The
  anti-lock-in argument is the bridge to model independence.
